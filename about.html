About Me

Hey there! Welcome to the "About Me" page, where I drop some of the professionalism from the home page so you can get a look at the woman who made the site you're viewing.

As previously mentioned, my name is Emma Pegram. But aside from coding, photography, digital design, and writing (all of which are presented on this website) there's a lot I enjoy. In particular, I love video games, Dungeons and Dragons, and marching band. Basically, a cocktail of interests that most would consider some degree of nerdy. Occasionally, I also write music, though in a very loose sort of format. My main passion, however, is writing. From short stories that never got finished to half-edited novellas to epic, high fantasy sagas, my experience with writing has taken me far and wide, exploring many different cultures, places, times, and even things about myself.

I hope this site gives you a bit more insight into who I am, and what I like to do. Shoot me an email or send a message to my discord if you'd like to chat. Or, if an employer of mine somehow finds this, take a peek at my resume. And again, thanks for stopping by :)
